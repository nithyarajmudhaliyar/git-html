Avengers Endgame

Avengers: Endgame (2019) is a superhero film that serves as the epic conclusion to the Marvel Cinematic Universe's Infinity Saga. After the devastating events of Avengers: Infinity War, where Thanos wipes out half of all life in the universe, the remaining Avengers regroup to find a way to undo the destruction.They come up with a daring plan: travel back in time to collect the Infinity Stones before Thanos can use them, and bring everyone back. The mission tests their courage, unity, and sacrifices — especially Tony Stark (Iron Man), who gives his life to save the universe.The movie ends with Captain America passing on his shield to a new generation, marking both an end and a new beginning for the Avengers.
