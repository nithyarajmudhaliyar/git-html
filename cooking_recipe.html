Veg Sandwich

A quick, tasty vegetarian sandwich loaded with fresh vegetables and a tangy spread â€” perfect for breakfast or a light meal.

Ingredients

  • 4 slices whole-grain bread
  • 1 small tomato, sliced
  • 1/2 cucumber, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  • Handful of lettuce leaves
  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise (or yogurt spread)

Instructions

  1. Lightly toast the bread slices until golden.
  2. Spread mayonnaise or yogurt spread on one side of each slice.
  3. Layer lettuce, cucumber, tomato, and red onion on two slices of bread.
  4. Season with salt and pepper, then top with the remaining bread slices, spread-side down.
  5. Cut the sandwiches in half and serve immediately.
